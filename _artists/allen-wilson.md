Allen is St. Louis' own very talented piercer.  He joined the Cousin Paul's team and brought piercing into the studio at the beginning of 2020.  His experience and commitment to high quality materials, clean technique, and creating a comfortable, welcoming space for his clients has made him a great fit.  Allen is a full-service piercer who provides custom work and will also support his clients all through the healing process.  

Minors will be given consideration on a case by case basis for some piercings.  Contact Allen with specific inquiries, portfolio images for more intimate piercings available upon request.
